Welcome to Board Game Helper.

A collection of tools and resources to help you get the most out of your favorite games!

General Board Gaming tools

Randomize Teams and Player Order
Assign each player to a random team and also set a random team order.
Flip a Coin
Flip a coin and see the result.
Turn and Action Timer
Customize up to three different timers to use for different gameplay actions.
Start a Draft
This tool will allow you to add your own list of draftable items, and start a draft. It is advised that you determine teams and player order before starting a draft.
Player Point Tracker
Keep track of player or team points using this tool.
