Why are these changes necessary?

HOLAS when played 1vs1 or 2vs2 is a much more combat focused game. Players are highly rewarded for playing aggressively and limiting the amount of resources and land their opponents can control. For this reason certain extremely effecive combat focused units/tokens/spells/factions needed a slight balance adjustment.

With this in mind, some units that provided almost no combat advantage needed a boost to their combat viability.

Fireball
  • Increase cost to 5 crystals from 4 crystals.
Possession
  • Increase cost to 5 crystals from 4 crystals.
Petrify
  • Can not be scribed.
Earth Quake
  • Can not be scribed.

Druid
  • Increase damage to 3 from 1.
Cultist
  • Increase cost of level 1 ability to 4 crystals.
  • Increase cost of level 2 ability to 8 crystals.
Balista
  • Decrease damage vs heroes to 1, down from 2.
  • Raned attack ability does not work while the balista is in the same region as your capitol.
Seige Tower
  • Increase movement to 2, up from 1.
  • Increase damage to 2, up from 1.

Wandering Knight
  • Increase cost to 2 minerals from 1 mineral.
Wizzard Tower
  • Discard this token after it is used. Draw 1 spell card per capitol level, then discard down to your max hand size.
