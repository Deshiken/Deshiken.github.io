Egg Hunt

This game mode involves hunting for egg tokens that are stored with towers you control. While you can earn points from other means, the main source of points will come from protecting the eggs you have and destroying enemy towers to steal their eggs.

  • Designate 3 land exploration tokens per player that represent egg tokens. Because the HOLAS expansion only comes with 4 egg tokens, you will need to repurpose some other land exploraton tokens as egg tokens for this game mode. You can do this by adding a sticker or just mutually aggreeing with other players which tokens will be used as egg tokens.
    # of Players Egg Tokens Non-Egg Tokens
    2 6 27
    3 9 35
    4 12 32
    4 with floating island 12 43
    5 with floating island 15 40
    5 with 6 player board 15 51
    6 with 6 player board 18 48
    6 with 6 player board and floating island 18 59
  • See the table above for how many egg tokens and non egg land exploration tokens will be used for various player counts. After setting aside the number of egg tokens required, randomly select the remaining needed land exploration tokens.
  • Mix the egg tokens together with the required number of non egg tokens and place the tokens using the standard game rules.

  • The Catipult unit's ability gives +1 damage (rather than +2) for each 3 minerals the unit's owner has.
  • The normal egg token rules apply. When you destroy an enemy tower or move onto a region that has an egg token or multiple egg tokens, you gain those tokens.
  • Any time you would gain an egg token, it is added to a tower you control. A tower can hold a maximum of two eggs on it.
  • If a player gains an egg token or multiple egg tokens and has no tower to place them on, they will distribute the egg token(s) onto any desert regions on the map of their choice. A player may not add a second egg token to a desert region until all other desert regions on the map currently have an egg token on them.
  • A player is not eliminated if their capitol is defeated. Instead the attacking player plunders the capitol and gains half (rounded up) of each resource type that the defending player has. Then the attacking army is then moved to their capitol.

Unless noted below, players do not receive points for any other actions or at the end of the game.

  • Gain 1 point at the end of each round for each egg on a tower you control.
  • Gain 1 point for winning a battle.
  • Gain 1 point for casting a spell.

Second Chance

This game mode helps prevent quick and sudden wins by attacking an opponents capitol.

  • A player is only defeated when their capitol is defeated and if that capitol is currently level 1. Otherwise, the capitol level is decreased by 1.
  • When a capitol level is decreased, the player that owns the capitol must discard down to their new spell card hand limit. The capitol's owner may keep any additonal towers they contol that exceed their capitol's allowed limit.
